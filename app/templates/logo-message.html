To use your IA Sport logos in the application, you need to save both the color and white versions of your logo to the static/images folder:

Instructions:

  1. Save the color version of your logo as logo.png in the following folder:
    c:\Users\Anderson Domingos\Documents\Projetos\iasport\software_v1\football-vision-analytics\app\static\images\
